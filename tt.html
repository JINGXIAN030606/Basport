Biography

LeBron Raymone James Sr. is an American professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Widely considered one of the greatest basketball players of all time, he is often compared to Michael Jordan in debates over the greatest basketball player ever. LeBron James has won four NBA championships, four NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP) Awards, and two Olympic gold medals. He has also been selected to 17 NBA All-Star teams.

Stats

  • Height: 6 ft 9 in (2.06 m)
  • Weight: 250 lb (113 kg)
  • Position: Small forward / Power forward

Accolades

  • 4× NBA Champion (2012, 2013, 2016, 2020)
  • 4× NBA Most Valuable Player (2009, 2010, 2012, 2013)
  • 17× NBA All-Star (2005–2021)
